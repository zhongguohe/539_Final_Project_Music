Artist Spotlight

Get to know the voices that defined and continue to shape Chinese pop music — their stories, styles, and standout tracks.

Jay Chou

周杰伦

Born in Taiwan in 1979, Jay Chou is widely regarded as the "King of Mandopop." He revolutionized Chinese pop by blending classical Chinese instrumentation with Western R&B, hip-hop, and rock. His unique mumbling vocal style was initially controversial but became his signature.

Pop R&B Hip-Hop Taiwan

Must-Listen Tracks

  • Dao Xiang (稻香) — Upbeat harvest anthem
  • Qing Hua Ci (青花瓷) — Chinese-style elegance
  • Ye Qu (夜曲) — Dark, cinematic R&B
  • An Jing (安静) — Heartbreak ballad classic
  • Shuang Jie Gun (双截棍) — Martial-arts hip-hop

Wang Leehom

王力宏

Chinese-American musician who coined the term "chinked-out" to describe his fusion of Chinese traditional music with pop, hip-hop, and R&B. A multi-instrumentalist and Berklee-educated artist, Leehom pushed the boundaries of what Mandopop could be.

Pop R&B Fusion Chinese-American

Must-Listen Tracks

  • Wei Yi (唯一) — Timeless love ballad
  • Gai Shi Ying Xiong (盖世英雄) — Chinked-out anthem
  • Ni Bu Zhi Dao De Shi (你不知道的事) — From the film "Love in Disguise" (恋爱通告)
  • Hua Tian Cuo (花田错) — Peking opera meets pop
  • Luo Ye Gui Gen (落叶归根) — Cultural identity ballad

JJ Lin

林俊杰

Born in Singapore, JJ Lin is one of the most gifted vocalists and songwriters in Mandopop. His music combines pop, R&B, electronic, and classical elements. Known for his incredible vocal range and emotional delivery, he has won multiple Golden Melody Awards.

Pop Ballad Singapore

Must-Listen Tracks

  • Jiang Nan (江南) — Dreamy pop masterpiece
  • Xiu Lian Ai Qing (修炼爱情) — Emotional ballad
  • Ke Xi Mei Ru Guo (可惜没如果) — Bittersweet regret
  • Bu Wei Shei Er Zuo De Ge (不为谁而作的歌) — Award-winning track
  • Dui De Shi Jian Dui De Ren (对的时间对的人) — Beautiful love song

David Tao

陶喆

Often called the "Godfather of Chinese R&B," David Tao brought American soul, jazz, and R&B sensibilities to Mandopop. His self-titled debut album in 1997 was groundbreaking, introducing a smoother, groovier sound to Chinese-language music.

R&B Soul Jazz Taiwan

Must-Listen Tracks

  • Pu Tong Peng You (普通朋友) — Classic heartbreak R&B
  • Ai Hen Jian Dan (爱很简单) — Smooth, soulful ballad
  • Sha Shou Jian (杀手锏) — Funky groove
  • Gui (鬼) — Edgy rock-R&B crossover
  • Yue Liang Dai Biao Wo De Xin (月亮代表谁的心) — Teresa Teng tribute

Jason Zhang

张杰

Rising to fame through Chinese talent shows, Jason Zhang is celebrated for his powerful high notes and energetic performances. He won the International Artist of the Year at the American Music Awards in 2014, bringing global attention to Chinese pop.

Pop Power Ballad Mainland China

Must-Listen Tracks

  • Zhu Guang (逐光) — Inspirational anthem
  • Tian Xia (天下) — Epic, cinematic pop
  • Ming Tian Guo Hou (明天过后) — Romantic ballad
  • Zhe Jiu Shi Ai (这就是爱) — Sweet love song
  • Shao Nian Zhong Guo Shuo (少年中国说) — Patriotic pop

G.E.M.

邓紫棋

Gloria Tang, known as G.E.M. (Get Everybody Moving), is a Hong Kong singer-songwriter who gained massive popularity through the TV show "I Am a Singer." Known for her explosive vocal power and genre-blending pop, she is one of the biggest female artists in Chinese music.

Pop Power Vocal Hong Kong

Must-Listen Tracks

  • Guang Nian Zhi Wai (光年之外) — Sci-fi pop anthem from Passengers
  • Duo Yuan Dou Yao Zai Yi Qi (多远都要在一起) — Heartfelt love anthem
  • Where Did U Go — Bilingual pop banger
  • Ju Hao (句号) — Fierce, empowering break-up declaration
  • A.I.N.Y. — Bilingual pop classic

Khalil Fong

方大同

A Hong Kong-based singer-songwriter who brought authentic R&B, neo-soul, and jazz to Mandopop. Influenced by Stevie Wonder and D'Angelo, Khalil Fong's music stands out for its organic instrumentation and warm, textured vocals.

R&B Neo-Soul Jazz Hong Kong

Must-Listen Tracks

  • San Ren You (三人游) — Tender, jazzy ballad
  • Ai Ai Ai (爱爱爱) — Catchy soul-pop
  • Hong Dou (红豆) — Beautiful Faye Wong cover
  • Te Bie De Ren (特别的人) — Heartfelt love song
  • Wu Guo (悟空) — From the album "Journey to the West"

Eason Chan

陈奕迅

A Hong Kong legend equally acclaimed in Cantopop and Mandopop. Eason Chan is known for his deeply emotional voice and incredible versatility. He has won numerous awards and is considered one of the greatest Chinese-language singers of all time.

Pop Ballad Cantopop Hong Kong

Must-Listen Tracks

  • Shi Nian (十年) — Iconic Mandarin ballad
  • Hao Jiu Bu Jian (好久不见) — Nostalgic classic
  • Wen Wen (稳稳的幸福) — Warm and uplifting
  • Gu Du Huan Zhe (孤独患者) — Emotional masterpiece
  • Fu Zhong Ren (浮夸) — Dramatic Cantopop classic

Stefanie Sun

孙燕姿

A Singaporean singer whose warm, crystalline voice defined early 2000s Mandopop. Stefanie Sun's debut was a massive success, and she quickly became one of the top-selling female artists in Asia. Her songs are known for their youthful energy and emotional sincerity.

Pop Ballad Singapore

Must-Listen Tracks

  • Tian Hei Hei (天黑黑) — Debut classic
  • Yu Jian (遇见) — Iconic film soundtrack
  • Lv Guang (绿光) — Upbeat pop anthem
  • Wo Huai Nian De (我怀念的) — Emotional ballad
  • Feng Zheng (风筝) — Metaphorical love song

Xu Song (Vae)

许嵩

Born in Hefei in 1986, Xu Song — known online as "Vae" — is the undisputed king of Chinese internet music. He writes, composes, arranges, produces, and sings all his own songs. His poetic lyrics and "Chinese style" indie pop earned him millions of fans before ever signing with a label.

Internet Pop Indie Chinese Style Mainland China

Must-Listen Tracks

  • You He Bu Ke (有何不可) — Warm, playful hit
  • Duan Qiao Can Xue (断桥残雪) — Poetic "Chinese style" classic
  • Su Yan (素颜) — Charming duet anthem
  • Qing Ming Yu Shang (清明雨上) — Elegant classical imagery
  • Ru Guo Dang Shi (如果当时) — Nostalgic love ballad

Silence Wang

汪苏泷

Born in Shenyang in 1989, Silence Wang is one of the "Internet Big Three" (网络三巨头). A self-taught musician who started by posting original songs online, he quickly became a campus sensation. His catchy melodies and sweet vocal style made him a favorite in KTV rooms across China.

Internet Pop Sweet Pop Mainland China

Must-Listen Tracks

  • Xiao Xing Xing (小星星) — Viral modern nursery rhyme
  • Bu Fen Shou De Lian Ai (不分手的恋爱) — QQ Music #1 hit
  • You Dian Tian (有点甜) — Sweet duet with BY2
  • Hou Hui Wu Qi (后会无期) — Bittersweet farewell anthem
  • Wan An (晚安) — Tender late-night ballad

Xu Liang

徐良

Born in 1987, Xu Liang is the third member of the "Internet Big Three." Known for his quirky, playful duets with female singer Xiao Ling, Xu Liang's songs blended catchy hooks with edgy, club-influenced production. His music dominated QQ Music charts and campus karaoke in the early 2010s.

Internet Pop Duet Mainland China

Must-Listen Tracks

  • Ke Guan Bu Ke Yi (客官不可以) — Playful, iconic hit
  • Huai Nv Hai (坏女孩) — Edgy club-pop banger
  • Hou Hui Wu Qi (后会无期) — Collab with Silence Wang
  • Na Shi Yu (那时雨) — Nostalgic slow ballad
  • Kao Shi Shen Me De Dou Qu Si Ba (考试什么的都去死吧) — Campus comedy anthem

Mayday

五月天

Taiwan's biggest rock band, formed in 1997. Mayday blends pop-rock anthems with deeply emotional lyrics about youth, friendship, and perseverance. Their stadium concerts draw hundreds of thousands of fans across Asia. Lead singer Ashin's vocals and songwriting define a generation.

Rock Pop-Rock Taiwan Band

Must-Listen Tracks

  • Wen Rou (温柔) — Defining 2000s rock ballad
  • Tu Ran Hao Xiang Ni (突然好想你) — Emotional reunion anthem
  • Xian Yu (咸鱼) — Inspirational underdog song
  • Wo Bu Yuan Rang Ni Yi Ge Ren (我不愿让你一个人) — Tender love promise
  • Shang Xin De Ren Bie Ting Man Ge (伤心的人别听慢歌) — Ironic up-tempo heartbreak

Li Ronghao

李荣浩

Born in Bengbu in 1985, Li Ronghao is one of mainland China's most talented singer-songwriters and producers. Known for his distinctive small-eyed look and warm, smoky voice, he writes, composes, and produces nearly all of his own music. His debut single "Model" became an instant classic.

Pop Singer-Songwriter Mainland China

Must-Listen Tracks

  • Mo Te (模特) — Smoky, philosophical debut hit
  • Li Bai (李白) — Catchy tribute to the Tang poet
  • Bu Jiang Jiu (不将就) — Film theme from "He Yi Sheng Xiao Mo"
  • Nian Shao You Wei (年少有为) — Nostalgic ballad about youth
  • Ma Ma (麻雀) — Tender metaphor about ordinary life

Joker Xue

薛之谦

Born in Shanghai in 1983, Joker Xue is one of China's most popular singer-songwriters. After years of setbacks, his comeback single "Actor" went viral in 2015, making him a household name. Known for his witty personality and heartfelt ballads, he dominates Chinese streaming charts.

Pop Ballad Mainland China

Must-Listen Tracks

  • Yan Yuan (演员) — Viral comeback mega-hit
  • Chou Ba Guai (丑八怪) — Self-deprecating love song
  • Gang Gang Hao (刚刚好) — Bittersweet breakup anthem
  • Ren Shi Ni Zhen Hao (认识你真好) — Warm friendship ballad
  • Xia Yu Le (下雨了) — Melancholy classic
